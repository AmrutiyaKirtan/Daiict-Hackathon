Professional Email to a Manager

Write a professional email requesting time off for personal reasons.

Sample Email:

Subject: Request for Personal Leave

Dear [Manager's Name],

I hope this email finds you well. I am writing to request time off from [start date] to [end date] for personal reasons.

I have already completed all my pending tasks and will ensure a smooth handover to [colleague's name] who has agreed to cover for me during my absence. I will also be available on email for any urgent matters that may require my input.

Please let me know if you need any additional information or if there are any concerns regarding this request.

Thank you for your consideration.

Best regards,
[Your Name]

Self-Introduction

Write a brief introduction about yourself for an interview (1-2 minutes when spoken).

Sample Self-Introduction:

"Hello, I'm [Your Name], a [your profession] with [X years] of experience in [your industry]. I graduated from [University] with a degree in [your field], and since then, I've been passionate about [key area of interest].

In my current role at [Current Company], I've successfully [mention 1-2 key achievements]. I specialize in [your key skills] and enjoy tackling challenges that involve [types of problems you enjoy solving].

Outside of work, I'm passionate about [hobbies or interests], which I believe helps me bring a creative perspective to my professional work.

I'm excited about this opportunity because [specific reason related to the company/role], and I believe my background in [relevant experience] makes me a strong candidate for this position."
